Blue

Blue is the color of the sky and sea. It is often associated with depth and stability. It symbolizes trust, royality, wisdom, confidence, inteligence, faith, truth, and heaven

Green

Green us the color of nature. It symbolizes growth, harmony, freshness, and fertility. Green has strong emotional correspondence with safety.

Red

Red us the color of fire and blood, so it is associated with energy, war, danger, strenght, power, determination as well as passion, desire, and love.
